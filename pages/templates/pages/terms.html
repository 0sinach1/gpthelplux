6. Escrow and Payment System

Luxa operates a secure escrow-based payment system to safeguard both customers and vendors.

How It Works:

Customers make payments securely through Luxa’s payment gateway.

Funds are held in escrow until the product has been delivered and verified.

After successful customer confirmation, payments are released to the vendor.

Luxa does not store full payment card details. All payments are processed through PCI DSS–compliant third-party processors.

Vendors are not permitted to self-deliver or use unauthorized couriers.
This ensures every order maintains the same verified chain of trust from listing to handover.
