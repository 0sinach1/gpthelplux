Luxa Vendor Agreement (Online Click-to-Agree Version)

By registering as a vendor on Luxa, you agree to the following terms and conditions. Please read carefully before clicking “I Agree.”

1. Introduction

This agreement governs your use of Luxa to list and sell products.

By proceeding, you confirm that you understand and accept these terms.

2. Vendor Verification

You must provide valid business identification and registration.

Luxa may verify your identity and ability to fulfill orders.

Accounts failing verification or violating policies may be rejected or suspended.

3. Product Listing & 3D Verification

Products must be uploaded following Luxa’s onscreen guidance for photos, lighting, angles, and file format.

Luxa creates the official 3D “hero image” for display.

Products that differ from the verified sample or are uploaded incorrectly will be delisted, delivery halted, and refunds issued immediately.

Products must be ready for pickup; unavailability may result in account suspension.

4. Payments & Escrow

Customer payments are held in Luxa’s escrow until delivery is confirmed.

Funds are released minus Luxa’s service fee ([Insert %]) after inspection and customer confirmation.

Refunds are issued if products are damaged, defective, or misrepresented.

Only the customer’s name is shared for order identification; other details remain private.

5. Delivery Handling

All deliveries are handled by Luxa or verified logistics partners.

Luxa enforces a two-stage inspection:

Pre-pickup inspection at the vendor location

Final inspection at customer delivery

Vendors must cooperate fully with inspections.

6. Product Exclusivity & External Sales

Vendors may sell products outside Luxa only after delisting.

Delisting is allowed only if no customer has made payment through Luxa.

Selling outside Luxa, especially involving personal delivery, is strongly discouraged.

Luxa will not be held liable for off-platform sales.

Any violation (selling while listed or after payment) may result in:

Account suspension

Temporary or permanent ban

Forfeiture of pending payouts

7. Vendor Incentives, Visibility & Referrals

Vendors gain promotion and visibility through Luxa, including:

Featured placement on homepage, category pages, and search results

Badges for “Featured Vendor” status

Promotion via newsletters, social media, and other marketing channels

These benefits are earned through performance, such as completing a minimum number of successful sales ([Insert Number]) and maintaining high service quality.

Vendors are encouraged to refer customers to purchase through Luxa, as it increases visibility, credibility, and access to a wider audience — without additional advertising cost.

Luxa recognizes that long-term exposure, brand building, and customer trust generated by on-platform sales and referrals is more valuable than cash or traditional incentives.

8. Automatic Refunds

If a product does not match the verified sample or 3D render, Luxa will halt delivery and issue an immediate refund.

Vendor payouts for such orders may be withheld or forfeited.

9. Ratings & Reviews

Vendor profile information (name, address, bio, product details, categories, profile picture, reviews, ratings, successful deliveries) may be publicly displayed.

Luxa may moderate reviews for accuracy and fairness.

10. Fees & Commission

Luxa deducts a service fee ([Insert %]) from each transaction.

Future fee changes will be communicated 14 days in advance.
